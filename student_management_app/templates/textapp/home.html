WHY CHOOSE US

College Campus

SVIMS's flagship project, its 1st Integrated Hi-tech Campus in India, located within Kolkata, West Bengal; the campus is spread over 12 acres of land. The basic infrastructure constitutes modern academic facilities, sports and recreational facilities, total computerization of the different campuses and departments of the Institution with networking facility. .

Canteen facilities

The cafeteria in the college are the hub of every student activity. Students throng the cafeterias and help themselves to a variety of tasty foods which of course comes at a very nominal rates. College Canteen provides delicious and hygienic food and very affordable prizes. South Indian, North Indian and Chinese Vegetarian foods are available.

Library

The library has a vast and comprehensive collection in:

  • BCA & BBA text books
  • Journals and Magazines
  • Books on religion, ethics and political science
  • large No. of Books (Volumes)
  • large No. of Titles

Laboratories

State of the art Hi-Tech Computer and other laboratories is designed to help students develop effective skills and capabilities in academic and professional settings. These labs focus on work environments and real life situations.
.

Annual Science Events

The “Splendora” is a platform which provides ample scope to the Engineering and management students and the learners of “STEM” (Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics) to communicate among themselves and this gala conglomeration.

Placement

The department is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support all the stages of placement process viz. pre-placement talk, written test, group discussion & interviews. The training and placement department imparts training to the students.
